French Fries

Description

French fries are pretty easy to make, but with this recipe, they're gonna be even crunchier and flavorful.

This recipe is cheap, easy to follow, and most importantly, tasty.

Ingredients

  • Time: 20 Minutes
  • Potatoes: 5-6
  • Rosemary Salt: to your liking

Steps

  1. Peel & cut up your potatoes
    • Use a serrated knife for a better result!
  2. Rinse the cut up potatoes, but do not soak them
  3. Simmer the potatoes in vinegar water for 10 minutes
  4. Shallow-fry twice
  5. You're Done! Serve the french fries!
