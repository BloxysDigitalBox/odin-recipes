Bread

Description

Tired of the plain store-bought bread, or just wanted to finally try to make one yourself? This recipe is for you.

This recipe is cheap, easy to follow, and most importantly, tasty.

Ingredients

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Water: 2 cups (Warm)
  • Active dry yeast: 1 teasp.
  • Honey OR Sugar: 1/4 cup
  • Active dry yeast: 1 teasp.
  • Salt: 2 tsp.
  • Cooking Oil (canola or vegetable): 2 tablesp.
  • Flour: 2 cups

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C)
  2. Prepare the dough
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Grease your bread bowl & let dough rise
  5. Cook dough in your oven
  6. You're Done!
