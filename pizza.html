Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe will make everyone wish they had another slice.

This recipe is cheap, easy to follow, and most importantly, tasty.

Ingredients

  • Time: 40 Minutes
  • Pizza Dough: 16 ounces
  • Preferred Pizza Sauce: 1/2 cup
  • Handful of Pepperoni slices
    • (or your preferred toppings)
  • Mozzarela Cheese: 12 ounces
  • Ground Black Pepper: 1/2 teasp.
  • Oregano: 1 teasp. (optional)
  • Flour for Rolling

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C)
  2. Roll out the dough
  3. Add sauce, cheese & toppings
  4. Cook the pizza
  5. Slice n' serve
